# Gold-Silver-Price-Vs.-Geopolitical-Risks
Gold & Silver Price Analysis vs Geopolitical Risk


1.Short Description / Purpose:
- An exploratory data analysis (EDA) project that examines the relationship between gold and silver prices and geopolitical risk indicators. The project aims to uncover patterns, correlations, and trends that explain how global uncertainty impacts precious metal prices.


2.Tech Stack:
- Python (Pandas, NumPy for Data Cleaning & Analysis)
- Matplotlib & Seaborn (Data Visualization)
- Jupyter Notebook (EDA & Analysis Workflow)
- Excel / CSV (Data Storage & Handling)


3.Data Source:
- The dataset was obtained from Kaggle and includes historical gold and silver prices along with geopolitical risk index data over time.


4.Features / Highlights:
1. Business Problem :
- Investors and financial analysts need to understand how geopolitical events influence safe-haven assets like gold and silver. However, without proper analysis, it is difficult to quantify the impact of global risks on commodity prices.

2. Goal of the Dashboard / Project:
- To analyze historical trends of gold and silver prices.
- To study the correlation between geopolitical risk and metal prices.
- To identify patterns that can support investment and risk management decisions.

3. Walkthrough of Key Visuals:
- Line charts showing gold and silver price trends over time.
- Line/area chart visualizing geopolitical risk index fluctuations.
- Correlation heatmap between risk index and metal prices.
- Scatter plots highlighting relationships between risk levels and price movements.
- Moving average charts to identify long-term trends and volatility.

4. Business Impact and Insights:
- Demonstrated that gold tends to act as a stronger safe-haven asset during high geopolitical risk periods.
- Identified periods where silver showed delayed or weaker correlation compared to gold.
- Helped in understanding market behavior during global uncertainty.
- Provided insights useful for portfolio diversification and risk hedging strategies.
